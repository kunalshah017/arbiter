Arbiter Logo Concepts

1. The Monolith

A heavy, high-contrast 'A' marking authority and evidence. The offset purple square represents the external AI brain injecting intelligence.

2. The Gate

Reflects Arbiter's core innovation: The validation gate. Data passes through strict analytical pillars before execution.

3. Neo-Candles

A brutalist take on OHLCV candlesticks ascending to the right. Expresses trading and growth in the signature color palette.

4. Signal Cross

Represents indicator crossovers (MACD/EMA) and converging data points being processed into a single centralized execution node.

5. Apex Validator

An abstract 'A' that doubles as an ascending chart. The focal yellow dot is the targeted trading edge.

6. Strategy Matrix

A balanced 2x2 grid signifying multi-agent coordination. The crosshair binds the modules into deterministic execution.
